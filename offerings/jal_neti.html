Jal Neti

Keeping your sinuses well-balanced and maintaining free movement within them is very important. Jala Neti is towards this – it reduces the mucus in the system. But this practice has to be properly imparted.

  • It relieves asthma, cold, and all kappa related problems
  • Helps with diseases of eyes, nose and throat
  • Removes excess mucus and pollutants from nasal passages and sinuses
  • Insomnia, tiredness, improves quality of sleep
  • Relieves cold, allergies, sinusitis
  • Relieves migraine and headaches, anxiety, stress release and brings tranquility
