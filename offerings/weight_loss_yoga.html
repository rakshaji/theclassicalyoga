Weight loss - The Yogic Way

Hatha Yoga in its original form is not an exercise form to gain fitness or lose weight but a methodology for a human being to flower into an ultimate possibility

Sadhguru

Sadhguru looks at how with yoga, the body naturally comes to its optimal weight. However, doing yoga for weight loss isn’t the best use of this phenomenal system.

WHAT DO WE DO?

We help people lose weight safely in a natural and sustainable way.

HIGHLIGHTS

  • Learn Hatha Yoga, Meditations & Pranayama
  • Trained ISHA Hatha Yoga teachers for guidance
  • Guided practice session
  • Committed space for your daily sadhana
  • Practice corrections by teachers
  • Insightful videos from Sadhguru
  • Food, nature awareness and much more!
