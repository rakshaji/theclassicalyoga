Dincharya

We are super excited to offer a holistic program where you have the opportunity to go through the guided sessions and establish yourself into yoga.

  • Learn Yoga, Meditations & Pranayama
  • Trained ISHA Hatha Yoga teachers for guidance
  • Guided practice sessions
  • Committed space for your daily sadhana
  • Practice corrections by teachers
  • Insightful videos from Sadhguru
  • Food, nature awareness and much more!
  • Age limit - 14+ years
  • Total 20, 1-Hour Sessions/Month
  • Dates declared beforehand by TCY for the entire month. September dates are 1, 2, 3, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, 19, 20, 21, 22, 23, 24, 26, 27, 28, 29, 30
  • TCY may reschedule few sessions as needed
  • Must attend mandatory sessions (if any)

Monthly Fee - ₹ 3000/month

You also have the option to choose from different batches at your convenience:

Morning 1st batch - 6:00 AM - 7:00 AM

Morning 2nd batch - 7:15 AM - 8:15 AM

** Empty stomach condition required: Due to the nature of the practices, please ensure you finish any heavy meal at least 4 hours before the class, snack 2.5 hours before and beverage (except water) at least 1.5 hour before the session.
