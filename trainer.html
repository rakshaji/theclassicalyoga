Join the Save Soil Movement

Why Save Soil?

Save Soil is a global movement launched by Sadhguru, to address the soil crisis by bringing together people from around the world to stand up for Soil Health, and supporting leaders of all nations to institute national policies and actions toward increasing the organic content in cultivable Soil.

In this urgent, global endeavor, we invite your support. The true success of this movement is to make humanity come together as one to secure the future of all life and our planet.

Bikash Singhal

Certified ISHA Hatha Yoga Teacher | Seeker | Software Engineer (M.S.)

Bikash completed his Bachelor’s in Computer Engineering from Institute of Engineering & Technology, Indore, India in 2008. Then he received an M.S. in Computer Science from San Diego State University, San Diego, California, USA in the year 2011. After that, he worked in the USA as a Software Engineer at Motorola from 2011 to 2013. And then, he led many projects in Qualcomm from 2013 to 2019.

Bikash started his spiritual journey in 2013 in a program called Inner Engineering with Sadhguru in Atlanta, USA. He was blown away by the sheer presence of Sadhguru in the program. Then he took other ISHA programs like Bhava Spandana, Shoonya, Guru Pooja, and Samyama.

And then there was no looking back for him. He took up the 21 weeks ISHA Hatha Yoga Teacher’s Training Program for about 1750 hours of training in 2020. Isha Hatha Yoga School delivers classical Hatha Yoga in its full depth and dimension where you get to learn the practices like Upa Yoga, Angamardana, Surya Kriya, Yogasanas, Bhuta Shuddhi, Surya Shakti, Mantra Yoga, Nada Yoga, Bhakti Sadhana and many Guided Meditations. As a certified teacher, Bikash wants to offer this ancient science in all its purity and make it available to everyone.

Raksha Singhania

Certified ISHA Hatha Yoga Teacher | Seeker | Software Engineer (B.E.)

Then she worked in Pune in many software companies like Bioimagene, Cognizant & Synerzip from 2008 to 2012. After that, she worked in USA as a Software Engineer at GoldenHour Data Systems from 2016 to 2018.

Raksha started her spiritual journey in 2015 with Inner Engineering Program in San Diego, California, USA which was a turning point in her life. Then she took other ISHA programs like Bhava Spandana, Shoonya, Guru Pooja, and Samyama.

To deepen her practices further and offer this ancient science to as many as possible, Raksha took up the Intense 21 weeks ISHA Hatha Yoga Teacher’s Training Program for about 1750 hours of training in 2020 and has been graduated to offer programs like Upa Yoga, Angamardana, Surya Kriya, Yogasanas, Bhuta Shuddhi, Surya Shakti, and many more.
