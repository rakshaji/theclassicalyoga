Studio Classes

When taught in the right kind of atmosphere, Hatha Yoga becomes a phenomenal process of making the body into a device to receive the Divine.

Sadhguru

TCY Studio is an unparalleled opportunity to learn practices derived from a yogic tradition maintained in its full purity and vibrancy for thousands of years, and to continue these practices in a dedicated space under the guidance of trained Classical Hatha Yoga teachers.

We offer regular guided sessions to deepen one’s sadhana. This studio have been maintained keeping in mind the importance of having a conducive atmosphere to enhance the experience of Isha Hatha Yoga practices.

Offering programs in an organised, high quality, and holistic format, TCY is an invitation to anyone and everyone seeking a transformation by making classical Hatha Yoga a part of their lives. We extend opportunities to everyone to join, build upon their learnings, and continue their sadhana regularly in the dedicated spaces provided by TCY.

All programs and guided sessions are conducted by trained teachers who have undergone 1750+ hours of rigorous training at the Isha Yoga Centre and are certified by the Isha School of Hatha Yoga. Under the guidance of Sadhguru, these teachers offer classical Hatha Yoga in its full depth and dimension.
