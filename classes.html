Classes

Home Classes

We offer these three class formats:

Group Sessions

The energy shared between everyone in class, whether you are doing yoga together or meditating as a group, there’s a certain feeling you get in a group class that you can’t get anywhere else. Plus, you can use that group to intensify your own practices and push yourself further!

Online Sessions

Let your home be your personal Yoga studio. With our community, you can stream yoga classes in your favorite device. An online class is a course conducted over the Internet. Without the need to arrive at a studio or getting stuck in traffic. Online yoga allows you to stay at home and commit to your practice in a way that is simple and easy.

Private Sessions

There’s also an option of attending private yoga classes or even find a male or female physical trainer that will incorporate yoga in your regular routine.
